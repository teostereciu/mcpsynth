# Add an Apple Pay domain
Adds the new domain to the list of Apple Pay domains that are registered with the merchant account and the payment method identified in the path. For more information, seeApple Pay documentation.
To make this request, your API credential must have the followingrole:
- Management API—Payment methods read and write
The unique identifier of the payment method.
The unique identifier of the merchant account.
The list of merchant domains. Maximum: 99 domains per request.
For more information, seeApple Pay documentation.
