## Ⅰ. Overview
The rules in this guide apply to cross-border partners(TikTok Shop Partners) with **their company entity (or branches) registered in Mainland China and Hong Kong** (companies based in Taiwan and Macau are not supported for the time being. Contact your operation manager for further information, if needed.) that work with creators, sellers, brands on TikTok Shop. TikTok Shop Partner types include, but are not limited to, TSP, MCN, affiliate partners (TAP), and others.
This document, as well as the onboarding rules, **will be updated periodically as the platform's business continues to develop. Please revisit these onboarding rules regularly** to ensure that your business is in compliance with the latest rules.

# Ⅳ. Required Materials
As part of the application process, cross-border partners must submit relevant business qualifications, as well as any other additional information and documents that may be required. Upon receiving the application, TikTok Shop will review the materials, and may require supplemental information and documents for a second round of review before approving applicants as **onboarded TikTok Shop partners**. Any personal data will be treated in accordance with TikTok's privacy statement.
