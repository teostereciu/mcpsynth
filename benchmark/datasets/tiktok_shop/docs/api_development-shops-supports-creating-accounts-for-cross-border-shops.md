# Development shops supports creating accounts for cross-border shops

*Source: https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/development-shops-supports-creating-accounts-for-cross-border-shops*

---

As of today, the Development Shops tool, formerly known as Sandbox Shops, supports creating test accounts for cross-border shops.
## Available markets
This update is available for TH, VN, SG, PH, MY, US and UK markets.
## Steps

1. From your Partner Center, click **Development Kits - Development Shops**
2. Click **Enter** and choose a market
3. On the "Manage accounts" page, click **Create test account**, and choose "Core function", then click **Next**
4. Then for the "Shop type", choose **Cross border**
